Our Faqs

What is the objective of Future Farmers?

Future Farmers is an initiative that aims to connect landowners and aspiring farmers. It is more over like "KUTHAGAI".

Who can participate in Future Farmers?

Future Farmers is open to both landowners willing to collaborate with aspiring farmers and individuals with an interest in agriculture but without access to land.

How can I offer my land as a landowner?

Landowners can post their property in this platform and create a listing to offer their land. You can provide details about your land and your contact details in this platform.

Is there a cost involved in using Future Farmers?

Future Farmers is a Non profit platform here we don't charge for any services,It is a platform to connect peoples.

I'm an aspiring farmer; how can I find land to farm?

You can visit our flatform and there is option called "Available Properties" , There you can choose a agriculture land for forming and you can get land owners details.

How I ensure a mutually beneficial with landowners?

Here we provide some details so You can directly contact the land owners and you can discuss about profits and shares.It is an one to one communication between you and landowners
